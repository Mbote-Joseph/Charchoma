From Our Farm to Your Fork

Our unique farm to grill concept is much more than just a food culture or restaurant, we are proud to be part of a social revolution.

Our organic home farm provides us with the majority of the delicious, freshest ingredients we use, anything we don’t have is sourced locally from farms around Mombasa, which our expert team of chefs then transform into the tastiest, African and international cuisines from our carefully curated menu and served daily to your table for you to enjoy.
